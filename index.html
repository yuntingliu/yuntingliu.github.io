I am an Assistant Professor of Finance in the Department of Finance, School of Economics at Peking University. I hold a Ph.D. (2017) and M.A. (2014) in Economics from the Johns Hopkins University and B.S. in Mathematics and B.A. in Finance with distinction from Wuhan University.

My research interests include Asset Pricing, Macro-Finance and Financial Econometrics
