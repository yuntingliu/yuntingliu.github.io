Yunting Liu is an Associate Professor of Finance (with tenure) at School of Economics, Peking University. He holds a Ph.D. (2017) and M.A. (2014) in Economics from the Johns Hopkins University and B.S. in Mathematics and B.A. in Finance with distinction from Wuhan University.

His research interests include Asset Pricing, Financial Economics and Macro-Finance
